Welcome! We are glad you are interested in career coaching.

REAP is a Niche boutique information technology career coaching service that provides transformational expert advice to walk you through various stages of your working adult lives.

We’ll team up with you to reach your goals. In the process you’ll gain new skillsets and strategies that will serve you in your current status and in your future plans.

Career Coaching
Searching for something new and better? Or feeling stressed, stuck or restless? You may have a “perfectly good” job but want something new — so that you can learn new things, have more fun, and exercise your gifts in new ways. Or you may be really unhappy and urgently want to find a new job. Learn more

Manage Career
Are you making the most of your career? Maybe you’re doing well, and want to get even better: learn new skills, have more impact, network better, become a more effective boss, and receive more compensation and recognition. Or you want a sounding board for a big decision. Learn more

Search Job
Are you using outdated methods to find a job, spending most of your time searching the web and sending out lots of identical resumes? Are your interviews not resulting in offers? Or aren’t you getting interviews? Is your networking not working? Do you use social networking to stand out? Learn more

Individual Coaching
Coaching is now considered a high level perk. Executives seek it, usually for business effectiveness. And many non-execs engage a coach for work-related or personal reasons, viewing it an investment in themselves. Coaching can also help you improve your life when things aren’t going well. Learn more
